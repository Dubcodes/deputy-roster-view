Help

How Deputy Roster View Works

Back to settings

What This App Is

This is a read-only copy of Deputy roster data. It is built to make your roster easier to read on a phone, tablet, or desktop.

It can lag behind Deputy, so check Deputy directly if something looks urgent, missing, or payroll-critical.

What You Should See

Your calendar, list, day pages, timesheets, notes, and change badges are scoped to your signed-in account.

Shared crew/schedule context can show other people working the same race day because that is the whole point of the crew table.

Admin pages can see user and diagnostic information needed to keep the system working.

Main Calendar

Month view
The default screen. It shows your rostered days, track, position, start time, changed badges, and fortnight timesheet markers.
List view
A more detailed month list. Use it when you want notes, locations, and hours without opening each day.
Previous / Next
Move one month backward or forward.
Month/List icons
Switch between the full calendar and the list view.
Gear icon
Opens Settings.

Day Page

Top card
Shows your track, vehicle if known, position, race type, address, and calculated hours.
Race Day
Shows the short roster note: trucks, office/Clow Place, on-track time, first cross/live/records, race count, first race, and last race where Deputy provides it.
Deputy Schedule
Shows the crew list for the same date and track. Position is on the left, person in the middle, vehicle on the right.
Changed badge
Means your shift changed since the previous sync. Tap/open the change area to see what changed.
Maths
Shows the quick start-to-finish total. Open it for the detailed calculation.
Notes and timing
Save private notes or enter a changed last-race/finished-back time for your own checking.

Settings

Sync my roster
Starts a Deputy sync for your account. Leave the page open if you want to watch the progress bar.
Theme
Changes your colour palette. It is saved to your user account.
Change PIN
Lets you change your local roster-viewer PIN. This is separate from your Deputy password.
Update Deputy login
Lets you re-enter your Deputy URL, email, and password if signup details were wrong or your Deputy password changed.
Logout icon
Untrusts this device. You will need your email and PIN to sign in again.
Report an Error
Saves your message plus recent redacted diagnostics so an admin can inspect what the app saw.
iCal Backup Feed
Optional. Paste your Deputy calendar URL if you want the iCal feed used as a fallback during sync.

Admin

Users
Admins can expand users, revoke trusted devices, update Deputy login details, reset PINs, deactivate users, and reset a user's local roster copy.
Error Reports
Shows reports submitted from Settings, including the user's note and redacted diagnostic data.
Clear All Changed Flags
Clears noisy change badges globally. Normal users only clear their own roster changes from Settings.

Shortcuts

Swipe left/right
Move between months on touch devices.
M
Switch to month view.
L
Switch to list view.
N
Next month.
P
Previous month.
S
Start a sync from the calendar page.

Little Things Worth Knowing

  • The clock icon on a Sunday opens the fortnight timesheet summary.
  • Open shifts are listed separately from your own rostered shifts.
  • Opening change history marks your own changed shift as viewed.
  • Trusted-device login is long-lived and refreshes when you use the app.
  • The app never writes back to Deputy.
