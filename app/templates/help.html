A quick note before you start

This app keeps a clearer, phone-friendly copy of the roster in Deputy. It does not change anything in Deputy.

Most of the time this is the quickest place to check your day. If something is missing, urgent, or important for payroll, check Deputy as well because a sync can be a little behind.

Your calendar

The calendar only shows shifts that belong to you. Available shifts are shown separately, so they should not be mistaken for work you have already been rostered for.

Calendar or list
The first button in the top-right corner switches between the month calendar and the month list. Its icon changes to show the view you can switch to.
Globe
Opens the shared crew calendar. This is useful for seeing which locations are in use when planning vehicle or equipment maintenance.
Person icon
Appears while you are looking at the shared calendar. Tap it to return to your own roster.
Previous and next
Move backward or forward by one month. You can also swipe across the calendar on a phone.
Coloured shifts
Colours are based on the location, so the same track keeps the same colour within your chosen theme.

Opening a day

Tap a shift to see the full day. The heading shows your location, vehicle when one is known, position, race type, and rostered hours.

Race Day
Shows the useful times from the roster note, such as trucks, office or Clow Place, on track, first cross, and the first and last race.
Crew schedule
Shows the positions, people, and vehicles for that location. A muted TBC means Deputy has a position for the day but has not given us a name.
Changed
Means something has changed since an earlier sync. Open Change History to see the old and new details. Reading it does not clear the badge.
Time calculation
Open the start-and-finish row to see how travel, the last race, pack-up, and the return trip were worked out. If the note and Deputy disagree, both times are shown so you can check them.
Your notes
Notes saved at the bottom of a shift are private to your account. You can also record a changed last-race time or when the day actually finished.
Track map
Some thoroughbred days have a 2D course map at the bottom of the page. It only appears when the app has confidently matched and saved the correct Love Racing map.

Planning and open shifts

Open
An outlined Open badge means Deputy has an available shift. It is not a confirmed shift and is not filtered by position.
Love Racing
A faded gold planning entry is a public race date, not a roster. Tap it to see the saved date, location, and source. Once Deputy has a real shift for that day and location, Deputy takes priority.

Timesheets

The small clock on a Sunday opens the fortnight summary. Days you did not work stay compact, while worked days show their hours and location.

Tap a day to open its full race-day page, or open Calculation to check the maths without leaving the timesheet. Any private note you saved for that day is shown there too.

Settings

Sync my roster
Checks Deputy for your latest roster. Syncs also run automatically, and accounts are staggered so everyone does not hit Deputy at once.
Theme
Choose the colours you prefer. Your choice follows your account across trusted devices.
Change PIN
Changes the PIN used for this roster viewer. It does not change your Deputy password.
Update Deputy login
Use this after changing your Deputy password, or if the email or password entered at signup was wrong.
Report an error
Tell an admin what looks wrong. The report includes recent redacted sync information so they have something useful to investigate.
iCal backup
An optional Deputy calendar feed that can add fallback details when a normal sync is missing something.
Logout icon
Removes trust from this device after you confirm. You will need your email and roster PIN to sign in again.

Keyboard shortcuts

M
Open month view.
L
Open list view.
N
Go to the next month.
P
Go to the previous month.
S
Start your roster sync from the calendar.
Swipe left or right
Move between months on a touch screen.

Good to know

  • Your trusted-device login lasts a long time and its expiry is refreshed while you keep using the app.
  • Opening a change badge never marks it as read or removes it.
  • The shared crew calendar does not show private notes, open shifts, or personal change badges.
  • Deputy remains the source of truth for confirmed shifts and rostered hours.
